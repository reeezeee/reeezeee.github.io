Information

Reece Bautista is an accomplished explorer, photographer, creative director, and journalist.
Traveling throughout the world in order to pursue the farthest expanses of Earth.
With a camera on hand, he aspires to capture stories that inspires our
generation to rekindle our relationship with nature.

Reece's visionary perspective has earned him many global projects with fortune 500 companies.
Through social media, Reece strives to share his visions of untamed,
powerful landscapes and compelling portraits with millions of people.

Background

Reece Bautista is a photographer who is based on Las Vegas, Nevada. He is currently a full-time student at UNLV - seeking a master's degree in Business Information Systems and Computer Engineering.

Experience

Reece have worked with travel agencies throughout the United States, Australia and Thailand. He started his international company from the groundup at the age of seventeen.
